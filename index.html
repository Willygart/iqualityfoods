About Us

We believe in a better world with natural resources, less pollution, healthier and happier people.

We have inherited the wisdom in agricultural and harvesting practices from our great ancient culture. Our varied ecosystem provides us a rich healthy biodiversity that allows us to offer the world the best superfoods and natural ingredients. In 2009 our company was born with the mission of improving people’s quality of life by producing healthy products promoting the preservation and respect of our natural resources.

We have a great team of specialists and strategic partners committed to one goal: to offer products that improve the quality of life of our customers.

CACAO

FULL MAGNESIUM

Cacao has high levels of magnesium for a healthy heart and brain.

RICH IN IRON

Cacao is the vegetable with the highest source of iron.

MOOD BOOSTER

Cacao is a natural mood booster and antidepressant.

Cacao is the fruit of a 30-foot tall native tropical tree. Cacao bean has antioxidants, fats, carbohydrates, proteins, polyphenols such as flavonoids, and minerals such as calcium, magnesium, sulfur, copper, iron, zinc, and potassium. It also contains oleic acid, a heart-healthy essential monounsaturated fat, fiber and vitamins E, B2, B1, B5, B3 and B9.

Origin

San Martin, Amazonas, Ucayali

PRESENTATION: Roasted nibs

ENERGY

Maca increases energy level and stamina.

WOMEN'S HEALTH AND MOOD

Maca relieves menstrual and menopausal problems.

MOOD BALANCE

Maca relieves symptoms of stress and depression.

Inca warriors consumed maca before going to battle for its energetic properties. Maca root has been cultivated in the Peruvian Andes for centuries, over 12,000 feet high feet. TARWI

HIGH AMOUNT OF PROTEIN

Tarwi contains 40% protein (per 100g).

RICH IN OMEGA 3 FATTY ACID

Tarwi is high in unsaturated fat.

HIGH CALCIUM CONTENT

Tarwin is a healthy alternative for vegans and lactose intolerant.

This Andean legume grows in the Andes and has been used as a food by Andean people since ancient times, especially in soups, stews, salads and by itself mixed with boiled maize. Tarwi is well known for its high protein content and that’s what makes it potentially useful as a protein source in the market. It also regulates blood glucose, improves the immune system and has a high content of calcium and fiber which helps improve digestion.

Goldenberry helps to prevent certain chronic diseases.

Lucuma has beneficial nutrients that sugar lacks.

Lucuma helps the digestive system work properly.

RICH IN IRON
